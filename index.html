Meet the
Characters

  • Dante Koryu
    Dante Koryu is a kind Blader with a cheery personality and a strong sense of justice. But, when he gets nervous, his intense expression can scare off those around him. He learned to Blade from his mentor, Valt Aoi, during his time at BC Sol. After witnessing Valt’s Valtryek achieve Hyper-Flux, the ultimate state of a Bey, Dante decides to set off on his own adventure to help his Bey, Dragon, discover its full potential.
    Find out more
  • Delta Zakuro
    Delta Zakuro is one of BC Sol’s top Bladers and widely regarded as Valt Aoi’s successor. Few can rival Delta’s passion for Blading, but this very passion causes Delta to be overly harsh on himself and others. He believes there’s only room for one star in the Blading sky, and it’s going to be him.
    Find out more
  • Arman Kusaba
    A Blader for the Victories Bey club, Arman Kusaba is a man of many faces. While typically easy-going and quick with a joke, he’s also known to analyze Beys like a professor, complete with a thick pair of glasses. Once in the heat of battle, he trades his glasses for a headband; gone is the scholar, replaced by a fierce fighter. Though he can be moody at times, Arman is kind to his friends, and for that reason he is well-liked by all.
    Find out more
  • Fumiya Kindo
    As captain of the elite Spark Devils Bey club, Fumiya Kindo looks down on Bladers he sees as less talented. He acts polite around opponents from whom he has something to gain, but once he considers them an enemy, he’ll do anything to crush them. While Fumiya plays the part of genius in front of others, he is actually a hard worker whose skills are all thanks to his intense training behind closed doors.
    Find out more
  • Valt Aoi
    BC Sol veteran and Blading legend Valt Aoi has bested countless rivals on his climb to the top of the Blading world. His sunny and larger-than-life presence never fails to make others smile. As the gold-standard for Bladers everywhere, Valt spends his days travelling the world, hosting tournaments and participating in exhibition battles.
    Find out more
  • Gwyn Reynolds
    Considered by many to be a mathematical genius, Gwyn has the ability to quantify any anomaly in an instant. Though normally holed up in his lab, he uses this ability and his newfound interest in Blading to create his own Bey, Genesis.
    Find out more
  • Arthur Peregrine
    As the self-proclaimed rightful king of Beyblade, Arthur forms his own Blading organization to challenge the official association. Fittingly for the wielder of the Bey Apocalypse, he seeks to bring about the end of the Blading world as we know it.
    Find out more
  • Joe Lazure
    Known as a bit of a wildcard, Joe Lazure usually decides his battle strategies with the flip of a coin. This might seem risky to some, but Joe is a skilled Blader and makes use of some tricky tactics. Try as they might, most people find him tough to dislike thanks to his devil-may-care attitude.
    Find out more
  • Aiger Akabane
    Bright, passionate, and friendly, Aiger Akabane is the reigning World Champion. His rivalry with Valt Aoi has become the stuff of legend. There was once a time when Aiger only sought power and almost lost his way. But thanks to his friends and his bond with Achilles, he was able to overcome the darkness within.
    Find out more
  • Lodin Haijima
    Lodin Haijima is a world-class decathlete who breaks records left and right whenever he competes in a sport. Now that he’s turned his sights to Blading, Lodin is dedicating himself mind, body, and soul to perfecting his launch and proving himself to be the best in the world.
    Find out more
  • Blindt Devoy
    As prideful as he is talented, Blindt DeVoy is a Blading artist and one of the Risen 3. He prizes beautiful Blading above all else, and has no time for sloppy, amateurish opponents. Blindt has taken up residence in an ancient castle, where he paints his opponents’ true forms before battling them in his gallery.
    Find out more
