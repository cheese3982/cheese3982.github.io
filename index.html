Meet the
Characters

  • Dante Koryu
    Dante Koryu is a kind Blader with a cheery personality and a strong sense of justice. But, when he gets nervous, his intense expression can scare off those around him. He learned to Blade from his mentor, Valt Aoi, during his time at BC Sol. After witnessing Valt’s Valtryek achieve Hyper-Flux, the ultimate state of a Bey, Dante decides to set off on his own adventure to help his Bey, Dragon, discover its full potential.
    Find out more
  • Delta Zakuro
    Delta Zakuro is one of BC Sol’s top Bladers and widely regarded as Valt Aoi’s successor. Few can rival Delta’s passion for Blading, but this very passion causes Delta to be overly harsh on himself and others. He believes there’s only room for one star in the Blading sky, and it’s going to be him.
    Find out more
  • Arman Kusaba
    A Blader for the Victories Bey club, Arman Kusaba is a man of many faces. While typically easy-going and quick with a joke, he’s also known to analyze Beys like a professor, complete with a thick pair of glasses. Once in the heat of battle, he trades his glasses for a headband; gone is the scholar, replaced by a fierce fighter. Though he can be moody at times, Arman is kind to his friends, and for that reason he is well-liked by all.
    Find out more
  • Fumiya Kindo
    As captain of the elite Spark Devils Bey club, Fumiya Kindo looks down on Bladers he sees as less talented. He acts polite around opponents from whom he has something to gain, but once he considers them an enemy, he’ll do anything to crush them. While Fumiya plays the part of genius in front of others, he is actually a hard worker whose skills are all thanks to his intense training behind closed doors.
    Find out more
  • Valt Aoi
    BC Sol veteran and Blading legend Valt Aoi has bested countless rivals on his climb to the top of the Blading world. His sunny and larger-than-life presence never fails to make others smile. As the gold-standard for Bladers everywhere, Valt spends his days travelling the world, hosting tournaments and participating in exhibition battles.
